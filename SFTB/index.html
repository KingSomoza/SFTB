Why SFTB exists

Idle games reward patience. Guilds should reward loyalty.

Most guilds are a leaderboard with a name attached. We built this one around the opposite idea: rank should reflect who actually helped the guild move forward, not who happened to start playing first.

Everyone starts at the bottom No inherited ranks, no favorites. Every member climbs the same ladder.
Activity over hype We track contribution and consistency, not who talks the loudest in chat.
Built to last This site, the roster, the stats — all of it exists so the guild outlives any one season.
